    Big Box collection

    PC Gaming Wiki

    Wiki for all kind of pc games. Targets to help making your pc-gaming experience more enjoyable. Like offering fixes for older games to make them even run on your system or adjusting them for modern resolutions. Many games lack certain settings or support for some older OS's.

    Website for reviews of movies or tv-series. What I like about this is that normal users and professional critics can give their review about the movie/series. I also prefer the UI over that of IMDB, which is a similar site. Finally there is now also a mobile-app available (09/2025).
